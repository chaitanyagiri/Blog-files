Just another humorous incident(rather say embarrassing) that happened to me.

https://www.scoopwhoop.com/

I came home after a long gap of 6 months and as soon as I came home I wanted to meet my friends but it got delayed as they had their end term examinations. This is the story of us meeting finally.

There are four of us, the classic high school buddies. Quick profiles:-

  • Chimta ( Yash )
  • Naag (Ankush)
  • Billa (Chhailesh)
  • Katora (Me)

Now I collected Billa and Naag and we planned to meet Chimta at his place and then decide where to go.

After an hour of discussion we all agreed upon Biryani. There were 3 options nearby so obviously we chose the 4th one 31 Kms away from the city. Chimta is good at driving(they say) so he was driving.

We were on highway, now Naag is a bit cocky and cautious so he told us this story of 2 guys getting killed in an accident when their car was surrounded by heavy trucks from the 3 sides and the truck at the back crushed their car. At the same moment the most obvious thing happens we somehow get surrounded by trucks on the three sides and every one is now recalling his story except Naag I asked “teri kyon nahi fat rahi hai?”(how are you so calm?) to which he replied “Jo do log mare the wo aage baithe the car mein piche wale zinda the!“(the two guys who died were sitting in the front). Laughter bursts in the car, now the trucks have cleared our way but as with all stories the laughter could not last longer front right tire got punctured after a horrifying sound “BOOM“.

The car went out of control and first went right and then extreme left and we are off the road. For the next 5 mins all of us were still as rock . I break the silence “had it happened while we were in between those trucks two of you would have been dead”.

Laughter bursts again.

Now after getting tire fixed “Biryani important hai babu” (Biriyani is important). Due to the incident we decided to stop by a temple to thank god for saving our lives. So we stopped by a “Sai Baba” temple. There was this one guy inside the temple who was singing devotional songs with harmonium he was using microphone to amplify his songs so it was audible all around the temple premises. As soon as I entered the temple I spotted a beautiful girl inside the temple. After we were done praying I felt it was my duty to make sure all of us spotted that girl. So I whispered hot chick at 3 o’clock but none were able to listen as the guy with harmonium was pretty loud. Then I again said hot chick at 3 o’clock still none heard me. So I decided to shout considering the songs will be loud enough and only they will hear me. So I shouted HOT CHICK AT 3 O’CLOCK! and at the very moment the guy stopped singing and all that anyone in the temple could hear was “HOT CHICK AT 3’O CLOCK!“. Everyone started looking at me with disgust I was very embarrassed. Before I could say a thing Chimta, Billa and Naag were gone.

I slowly walked out of the temple with my head staring at my foot constantly till I made my way to the car. So on that day I ate Biryani, terror and humiliation.
